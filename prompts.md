# Image Generation Prompts

## Base Template
The standard format used for all philosopher portraits to ensure visual consistency is:

`Oil painting portrait of [Philosopher Name], [Role/Description], [Specific Visual Details], classical style.`

## Specific Prompts Used

### Eastern Philosophers
- **Confucius**: `Oil painting portrait of Confucius, ancient Chinese philosopher, wise expression, traditional Hanfu robes, scroll in hand, soft lighting, classical style.`
- **Rumi**: `Oil painting portrait of Rumi, Persian poet and Sufi mystic, spiritual expression, turban, whirling dervish background hint, warm colors, classical style.`
- **Al-Ghazali**: `Oil painting portrait of Al-Ghazali, Islamic philosopher and theologian, scholarly appearance, turban and robes, holding a book, golden age of Islam style.`
- **Laozi**: `Oil painting portrait of Laozi, ancient Chinese philosopher, founder of Taoism, white beard, riding a water buffalo, soft misty background, classical style.`
- **Sun Tzu**: `Oil painting portrait of Sun Tzu, ancient Chinese military strategist, wearing armor, stern expression, holding a scroll, classical style.`
- **Buddha**: `Oil painting portrait of Siddhartha Gautama Buddha, spiritual teacher, meditating under Bodhi tree, serene expression, golden light, classical style.`
- **Zhuangzi**: `Oil painting portrait of Zhuangzi, ancient Chinese philosopher, dreaming of a butterfly, nature background, whimsical but wise, classical style.`
- **Mencius**: `Oil painting portrait of Mencius, Chinese Confucian philosopher, scholarly robes, teaching gesture, ancient china background, classical style.`
- **Nagarjuna**: `Oil painting portrait of Nagarjuna, Buddhist philosopher, monk robes, teaching gesture, emptiness symbolism, classical style.`
- **Mozi**: `Oil painting portrait of Mozi, Chinese philosopher, simple worker clothes, stern expression, fortifications in background, classical style.`
